About:

Spongebob is a yellow sponge that lives in a pineapple under the sea. He's a happy-go-lucky guy who, on occassion, gets himself into mischief. He enjoys his job at the Krusty Krab where he works with his neighbor Squidward and is bossed around my Mr. Krab. Most of all, he loves his best friend Patrick the star who lives under a rock.

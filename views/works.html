Spongebob is a very prosperous sponge, some would say the most prosperous of all. Some of his major achievements include:

  • Has become a local singer/superstar, rocking the socks off all of Bikini Bottom's residents.
  • Constantly makes employee of the month at the critically-acclaimed Krusty Krab, where he single-handedly serves all of Bikini Bottom the gastronomical delight that is the Krabby Patty.
  • Has has, thus far, managed to succesffuly dodge all of Plankton's evil schemes all with a smile of his face.
